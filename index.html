About Me

Frontend developer

I am Frontend developer

I specialize in HTML layout design and utilize various software and editors such as Sublime Text, VS Code, Chrome Dev Tools, OpenServer, Photoshop, Avocode, and Figma to create visually appealing and functional websites. I am well-versed in HTML5 and CSS3, including the use of preprocessors like Pug and SASS. Additionally, I have a strong command of Git for version control.

My approach to web development follows the principles of cross-browser compatibility and responsive design, using techniques like grid and flexbox. I prioritize semantic markup and accessibility, ensuring that websites are usable for all users. In the past, I have worked with the Bootstrap CSS framework to expedite development.

I am experienced in adapting and integrating layouts from design files (.psd, .fig) into the WordPress CMS. This involves converting the design into a fully functional WordPress theme.

JavaScript is an essential part of modern web development, and I incorporate it into my projects as needed. For tasks such as implementing galleries and lightboxes, I leverage jQuery and its corresponding plugins, configuring them to suit the project requirements. To streamline the development process and optimize code, I rely on the Gulp task manager.

I constantly stay updated with the latest technologies and strive to incorporate them into my projects. My goal is to provide high-quality products that meet the needs of my clients.
